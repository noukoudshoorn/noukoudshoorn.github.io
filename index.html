At the moment, I am a PhD candidate in the Knowledge Based Systems group at the Technische Universität Wien, under the supervision of Magdalena Ortiz. Our research mainly focusses on building the mathematical foundations for the SHACL standard, as presented by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). For this, we work on knowledge representation, query ansering, description logics and more. This project is part of the LogiCS-COFUND doctoral programme. Moreover, I am interested in, among others, automata theory, proof theory, fixed point logics and the history of philosophy.
